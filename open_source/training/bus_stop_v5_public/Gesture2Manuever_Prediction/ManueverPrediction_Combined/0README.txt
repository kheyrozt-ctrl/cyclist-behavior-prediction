ManueverPrediction_Combined contains the Stage2 maneuver classifier.
ManueverPrediction_Combined 存放 Stage2 maneuver 分类器。

The main entry point is `main.py`.
主入口文件是 `main.py`。

The Stage2 labels are `straight`, `yield`, and `overtake`.
Stage2 标签为 `straight`、`yield`、`overtake`。

The fold/subset split CSV files are stored in `ManueverDataset/`.
fold/subset 划分 CSV 文件存放在 `ManueverDataset/`。

The dataloader reads the pkl files generated by `../VideoCrop/ManueverCrop_combine.py`.
dataloader读取 `../VideoCrop/ManueverCrop_combine.py` 生成的 pkl 文件。

The combined model calls the frozen head, upper-limb, and leg Stage1 models online inside `forward()`.
组合模型会在 `forward()` 内在线调用头部、上肢和腿部 Stage1 模型。

The online hidden features are concatenated, projected, and passed into the Stage2 BiLSTM classifier.
在线 hidden feature 会被拼接、投影，然后输入 Stage2 BiLSTM 分类器。
